    About CloudG

    We’re a bunch of gamers located across the planet working together to provide a top-notch service that gives gamers the features they want.

    Mission Statement

    Our core mission is to use our innovative technology to provide gamers with freedom and choice in how they experience their favorite games. We are committed to breaking down the barriers that keep gamers from playing the games they own the way they want to play them, and to building a positive community of people who share our love for gaming.

    Do more. Give back.

    Game streaming is a new and challenging space, and we are committed to serving our community now and in the future through communication and transparency.

    Our vision

    We make our milestones and vision for the future of PC gaming clear and strive to meet the demand of our users.

    Open source

    We release open source projects we feel can help developers create better software—seeding the future for more creations.

    Community

    We have an active community of gamers. If you want to chat with our community, join our discord today.
